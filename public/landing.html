Welcome to CryptoGraphix

Our mission ‐ to inform and educate application designers and developers about secure application development techniques and cryptographic technologies

We're building Cryptographix to be a hub for developers and engineers who care about application security ‐ front-end, back-end, mobile or embedded.

Our focus is helping you to build more secure products and systems, so whether you're just curious, taking your first steps in application security, actively design and develop applications or are a seasoned profissional, we aim to have something for everyone: Take a look at what we are planning:

CRYPTOEXPLORE Explore a world of information, articles and writeups, links, questions & answers, ideas, problems and solutions. Interact with experts and peers from the Cryptographix community
CRYPTOLAB Online tools to create, test, debug, inspect and visualize security and cryptographic-related data. Experiment with cards, tokens, cryptograms, keys, certificates, transactions, and much much more
CRYPTOLIBRARY Browse our knowledge-base, crypto-dictionary, articles, books, videos, links, tutorials, lessons and a lot more. Search, filter and organize results by skill-level, topic or keyword
CRYPTOSCHOOL Learn and improve with our tutorials and online courses. Our focus is on practical knowledge and self-paced learning of techniques and methods for designing, developing and testing secure applications.

Here's a few of the topics we'll be covering

APP SECURITY Secure Applications are Secure by Design, built using Secure Dev Methods, and Tested for Security DESIGN PATTERNS Proven methods, designs and blueprints. Learn from the experts - make new mistakes but not old ones BUILDING BLOCKS Layered Security combining Cryptographic Primitives, Security Services and
Secure Components APPLICATIONS A world of applications and uses - Payments, Signatures, Communications, Data Protection and lots more ATTACKS & DEFENSES White-hat techniques to protect against the bad guys. To defend, you must know how to attack
VALIDATION Black, grey and white-box testing. Frameworks, Tools and Techniques for validation of secure components SECURE PLATFORMS Hardware and Software. Secure Elements and Smart-cards. Libraries, Components and Operating Systems HISTORICAL Cryptography and information security through the ages. Epic successes and failures. Technology and Evolution PERSPECTIVES Opinions, views and critical thinking. Stories and Cases. Ideas, Vision and Insights. Futurology PROFESSIONAL App-Security and Crypto Engineering as a Profession. Jumpstarting your career.
